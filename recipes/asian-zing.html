Slow Cooker Asian Zing Chicken Noodles

Description

These slow cooker Asian zing chicken noodles are amazing and full of flavor with very little effort. Chicken thighs are cooked slowly in a sweet and spicy chili, soy, and ginger sauce, then shredded and mixed with fresh lo mein noodles.

If you like spicy comfort food, this dish is a great choice. It has a strong and delicious flavor, and it is very easy to make, making it perfect for a simple homemade meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) jar Buffalo Wild Wings Asian Zing Sauce
  • 1 1/2 pounds skinless boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 (16 ounce) package fresh lo mein noodles
  • 1 bunch grenn onions, sliced, for garnish

Steps

  1. Place chicken thigs into a slow cooker.Pour sauce over chickenn and cover with the lid. Cook on low until chicken is very tender, about 3 hours.
  2. Use two forks to shred chicken into large chunks
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; add noodles and cook until tenderwith a bite, 40 seconds to 3 minutes, or according to package directions; drain. Place prepared lo mein into the crockpot with chicken and toss until combined.
  4. Serve with freshly sliced green onions.
